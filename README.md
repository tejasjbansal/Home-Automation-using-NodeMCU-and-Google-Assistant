# Home-Automation-using-NodeMCU-and-Google-Assistant

Home automation is part of "The Internet of Things," also known as IoT. The way devices and appliances can be networked together to provide us with seamless control over all aspects of your home and more.

# PARTS REQUIRED
* NodeMCU
* Relay Board
* Jumper Cables
* Bread Board
* Smartphone.

# Steps Required
* Downloading and Installing the Blynk App on the Smartphone.
* Uploading the code to NodeMCU.
* Hardware Assembly.
* Connecting Google Assistant (using IFTTT) to make the NodeMCU work with voice commands.
